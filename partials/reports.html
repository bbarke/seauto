Reports

The different frameworks have different ways reports are created.

  • JBehave
  • Cucumber
  • JUnit

HTML reports are generated at the end of the test results, The index is located in target/jbehave/view

The reports are generated like regular junit tests, located in target/surefire-reports

Reports that are generated for the JUnit sample project are plain old junit reports. They are located in target/surefire-reports and screenshots are located in target/screenshot
